We use technology such as "cookies" to collect information and store your online preferences. If you do not accept cookies being used, you can turn off cookies in the security settings of your web browser. You can also set your web browser to ask you each time our portal tries to place a cookie on your computer. You can also use your web browser to delete previously stored cookies. See your web browser’s help pages for more information. Furthermore, you can at any time delete cookies manually from your hard drive. Please note that if you choose not to accept cookies, functionality may be limited for some parts of the website and the website may not work as intended, e.g. you will not be able to save your wish list products.
