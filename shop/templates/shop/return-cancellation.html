Cancellation & Return Policy

Providing one of the best customer experience is our top most priority. We strongly recommend to read our Cancellation and return policy.

1. Cancellation of Subscription plan:

  • On cancellation on any account, customer need to submit the physical disc within 48 hours in same condition as it was provided by GameHunter, else cancellation request won’t get processed.
  • We will charge membership fees for next 15 days starting from cancellation request date.
  • Refund will get initiated within 48 hours and reflect in your bank account within 5-7 business days (as per bank’s policy), once we receive the game CDs at GameHunter facility.
  • Refund of security is subjected to quality check of the gaming CDs.

2. Cancellation of orders:

  • Cancellation of any order can be done within 24 hours of order placed.
  • Refund of cancellation will be initiated within 48 hours by same mode of payment and refunded amount will reflect in your account within 5-7 business days (as per bank’s policy, if paid online).
  • If package gets shipped, no cancellation request will be entertain.

3. Return for Subscription plan:

  • Return policy is not applicable on subscription plan.
  • If any customer do not like the game, they can exchange the game at any point of time.

Return of orders:

  • Returns are depend on different game categories, we request you to please check the product detail page for returnable and non-returnable category.
  • Any returns made should have proper packaging, if seal of any game is tampered, return request will not be entertained.
  • Refund will get initiated within 48 hours and reflect in your bank account within 5-7 business days (as per bank’s policy), once we receive the game CDs at GameHunter facility.
  • Return is subjected to quality check.
