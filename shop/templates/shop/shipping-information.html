Shipping Policy

With complete team efforts, we are able to cover more than 25, 000 PIN Code Pan India. We believe to reach all places in India.

Logistic Partner:

We use Multi brands logistic services to deliver your games. The delivery partner is selected according to delivery time and PIN code. We always wish to provide fastest door to door delivery.

Logistic Time:

We work on standard protocols. GameHutner delivery time depends on your area Pin code and state zone.

  • 1. Zone A: 1 - 2 Days Delivery.
  • 2. Zone B: 2 - 3 Days Delivery.
  • 3. Zone C: 3 - 4 Days Delivery.
  • 4. Zone D: 4 - 5 Days Delivery.
  • 5. Zone E: 5 – 6 Days Delivery.

Reverse Pickup:

We provide reverse pickup of the products on maximum of the serviceable PIN Codes. In case you’re area PIN code do not fall under serviceable PIN Code, customer need to send the product by their own.

Delivery Charges:

Delivery charges vary as per the product you purchase and would be communicated clearly at the time of checkout.

Note: There will be no delivery charges for Subscribers of Gaming library.

Order Tracking:

You will receive notification over email regarding tracking details of the products. Email will have link to track your products.

Cash on Delivery Charges:

Cash on delivery charge may applicable on different area PIN Codes and would be communicated clearly at the time of checkout.

Note: Gaming Subscription is not applicable for cash on delivery model. You need to make online payment to avail subscription.
